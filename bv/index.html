John 3:16 For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life.

Ephesian 2:9 not by works, so that no one can boast.

John 6:47 I tell you the truth, he who believes has everlasting life.

生活金句

約翰一書 4:7 親愛的弟兄阿、我們應當彼此相愛；因為愛是從　神來的；凡有愛心的、都是由　神而生、並且認識　神。
John-1 4:7 Dear friends, let us love one another, for love comes from God. Everyone who loves has been born of God and knows God.

約翰一書 4:8 沒有愛心的、就不認識　神；因為　神就是愛。
John-1 4:8 The one who does not love does not know God, for God is love.

希伯來書 4:16 所以我們只管坦然無懼的、來到施恩的寶座前、為要得憐恤、蒙恩惠作隨時的幫助。
Hebrews 4:16 Let us then approach the throne of grace with confidence, so that we may receive mercy and find grace to help us in our time of need.

以弗所書 5:15 你們要謹慎行事、不要像愚昧人、當像智慧人；
Ephesians 5:15 Be very careful, then, how you live--not as unwise but as wise,

以弗所書 5:16 要愛惜光陰、因為現今的世代邪惡。
Ephesians 5:16 making the most of every opportunity, because the days are evil.

以弗所書 5:17 不要作糊塗人、要明白主的旨意如何。
Ephesians 5:17 Therefore do not be foolish, but understand what the Lord's will is.

以弗所書5:18 不要醉酒、酒能使人放蕩、乃要被聖靈充滿。
Ephesians 5:18 Do not get drunk on wine, which leads to debauchery. Instead, be filled with the Spirit.

以弗所書2:20 並且被建造在使徒和先知的根基上、有基督耶穌自己為房角石；
Ephesians 2:20 built on the foundation of the apostles and prophets, with Christ Jesus himself as the chief cornerstone.

以弗所書2:21 各〔或作全〕房靠他聯絡得合式、漸漸成為主的聖殿；
Ephesian 2:21 In him the whole building is joined together and rises to become a holy temple in the Lord.

馬太福音28:19 所以你們要去、使萬民作我的門徒、奉父子聖靈的名、給他們施洗；〔或作給他們施洗歸於父子聖靈的名〕
Matthew 28:19 Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit,

馬太福音28:20 凡我所吩咐你們的、都教訓他們遵守我就常與你們同在、直到世界的末了。
Matthew28:20 and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age.

雅各書4:7 故此你們要順服　神；務要抵擋魔鬼、魔鬼就必離開你們逃跑了
James4:7 Submit yourselves, then, to God. Resist the devil, and he will flee from you.

彼得後書3:13 但我們照他的應許、盼望新天新地、有義居在其中。
Peter-2 3:13 But in keeping with his promise we are looking forward to a new heaven and a new earth, the home of righteousness.

彼得後書3:14 親愛的弟兄阿、你們既盼望這些事、就當殷勤、使自己沒有玷污、無可指摘、安然見主。
Peter-2 3:14 So then, dear friends, since you are looking forward to this, make every effort to be found spotless, blameless and at peace with him.
